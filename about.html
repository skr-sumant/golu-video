Our Camera Gear

At Golu Photography, we use the latest professional-grade camera equipment to ensure the highest quality images. Our primary camera bodies are the Nikon D7500,D7000 and the Sony NX100,NX200, which offer exceptional image resolution, low-light performance, and advanced autofocus capabilities.

For lenses, we rely on a variety of high-quality Nikon and Sony glass, including the Nikon 24-70mm f/2.8, the Nikon 70-200mm f/2.8, and the Sony 85mm f/1.4 GM. These lenses allow us to capture a wide range of shots, from sweeping landscapes to intimate close-ups, all with stunning clarity and depth of field.

Our History

GOLU HD Video was established in 2007, and since then, we have had the privilege of capturing the wedding days of hundreds of couples throughout California and beyond. From vibrant Hindu ceremonies to elegant Sikh weddings, we have honed our skills and developed a unique approach that allows us to create timeless, authentic, and visually stunning images for our clients.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for our craft have earned us a reputation as one of the top Indian wedding photography studios in the region. We are honored to be a part of our clients' most cherished moments, and we look forward to many more years of creating beautiful memories for couples to treasure.
